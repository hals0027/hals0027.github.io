About Me

Hi, my name is Kaila Page and I am a 19 year old student in the IMD program at Algonquin College. I was originally planning on joining the photography program, however it was cut the day after I found out that I had been accepted! It was super frustrating having waited for them to review my portfolio just for them to cancel the program. I am now hoping to use that turn of events to educate myself further into media and design.

In my spare time, I enjoy laying in bed and watching movies, although after watching all of favourites for the 5th time, it can get a bit bland. My favourite movie of all time is Damien Chazelle's "La La Land"; I could probably watch it a thousand times and never get sick of it though! It's such a wonderful movie, and although the ending just about kills me, I think it is one of the most perfect endings in the history of cinema, and it serves as a reminder to keep chasing your dreams.

Here's to the fools who dream, foolish as they may seem -Mia Dolan, La La Land
